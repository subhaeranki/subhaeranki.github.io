Introduction

The Clay Collective

The Clay Collective is is a maker-focused magazine dedicated to the art of ceramics and pottery. It features studio tours, artist profiles, glaze and tool tips, and cultural stories that highlight the craft's timeless appeal. With practical guides and creative inspiration, The Clay Collective offers a welcoming space where potters and enthusiasts can connect, learn, and celebrate the world of clay.
