Welcome to Sunflower's Cat Cafe!

Step into a haven where coffee and cuddles collide! At Sunflower's, we blend the joy of delicious beverages and bites with the heartwarming company of adorable, adoptable cats. Whether you're here for a quiet moment, to make a furry friend, or just to sip a perfectly brewed cappuccino, our cozy space is designed to make you smile.

All of our resident cats are cared for with love and are looking for their forever homes. So come for the coffee, stay for the purrs, and leave with unforgettable memories (and maybe a new family member!).
